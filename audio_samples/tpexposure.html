Exposure string for word segmentation using transitional probabilities

A sample of exposure string composed of 4 trisyllabic artificial words. This 25-second segment was excised from the original file created and made publicly available by Vuvan and Wojcik (2020).

Vuvan, D. T., & Wojcik, E. H. (2020). A free and publicly available stimuli set for statistical learning experiments in language and music. https://osf.io/t3p25
